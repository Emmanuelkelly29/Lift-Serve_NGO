About Lift and Serve All Foundation

Lift and Serve All Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to transforming the lives of children in underserved communities through education. We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to learn, grow, and achieve their dreams, regardless of their background or circumstances.

By lifting young minds with access to quality education and serving communities with care and support, we aim to break barriers, reduce inequality, and create brighter futures.

Our Journey

The Beginning

The establishment of Lift and Serve All Foundation is driven by a vision to uplift the vulnerable and those living in disadvantaged communities by granting them access to quality education and opportunities for growth.

As recorded in the scripture, "The glory of God is intelligence, or, in other words, light and truth." Guided by this divine principle, we believe that by bringing light and truth through education, we can help transform lives, strengthen families, and build stronger communities.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

"A world where every child, regardless of background or location, has equal access to education, the chance to unlock their potential, and the support to build a brighter future for themselves and their communities."

Our Programs

We run comprehensive educational programs designed to support vulnerable children and underserved communities.

Scholarship Support

Comprehensive scholarship programs covering tuition fees, school supplies, and educational expenses for children in need.

  • Full and partial tuition coverage
  • School supplies and uniforms
  • Transportation assistance
  • Academic support and tutoring
Impact: 200+ students currently supported

School Supplies Donation

Regular drives to provide essential learning materials to students who cannot afford them.

  • Textbooks and workbooks
  • Writing materials and stationery
  • Digital learning tools
  • Educational games and resources
Impact: 1,000+ students equipped last year

Community Learning Centers

Safe, well-equipped learning spaces in rural communities where educational infrastructure is lacking.

  • Modern learning facilities
  • Computer labs and internet access
  • Library and reading corners
  • After-school programs
Impact: 5 centers established

Mentorship & Skills Training

Guidance and skill development programs to help young people build confidence and prepare for their futures.

  • One-on-one mentorship
  • Career guidance and counseling
  • Life skills workshops
  • Leadership development programs
Impact: 150+ youth mentored

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs, donations, volunteering, and more.

Donations

How can I donate from abroad?

We accept global donations securely via Flutterwave and Paystack. You can also make bank transfers to our foundation account. All donations are tax-deductible and we provide receipts.

How do I know my donation is being used properly?

We provide regular transparency reports showing how donations are allocated. You can view our annual financial reports, impact updates, and program evaluations on our website.

Can I make a recurring donation?

Yes! We offer monthly recurring donations that provide consistent support. You can set up recurring donations through our online donation form or contact us directly.

What is the minimum donation amount?

There is no minimum donation amount. Every contribution, no matter how small, makes a difference. Even $25 can provide school supplies for one child for a month.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, all donations to Lift and Serve All Foundation are tax-deductible. We provide official receipts for all donations that you can use for tax purposes.

Volunteering

Can I volunteer if I'm outside the country?

Yes! You can volunteer virtually through online tutoring, content creation, translation services, and technical support opportunities from anywhere in the world.

What skills do I need to volunteer?

We welcome volunteers with various skills and backgrounds. Whether you have teaching experience, technical skills, or simply a passion for education, there's a volunteer opportunity for you.

How much time do I need to commit?

Time commitments vary depending on the volunteer role. Some opportunities require just a few hours per week, while others are project-based. We're flexible and can work with your schedule.

Programs

How do you select students for scholarships?

We select students based on financial need, academic potential, and community recommendations, including family interviews and school assessments.

Can I sponsor a specific student?

Yes! We offer individual student sponsorship programs where you can support a specific child's education and receive regular updates about their progress.

What communities do you serve?

We primarily serve rural and underserved communities, focusing on areas where educational infrastructure is lacking. We've reached over 25 communities and continue to expand.

General

Is Lift and Serve All Foundation a registered organization?

Yes, Lift and Serve All Foundation is a registered non-profit organization. We operate with full legal compliance and maintain all necessary registrations and certifications.

Do you accept in-kind donations?

Yes! We accept books, school supplies, computers, tablets, and other learning materials. Contact us to discuss what items we currently need.

How can I stay updated on your work?

Subscribe to our newsletter, follow us on social media (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube), or visit our News & Blog section regularly.
